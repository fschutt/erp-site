### **Sales & Customer Relationship Management (CRM)**

*   **Customer & Lead Management:** Create, view, and manage a central database of your customers, leads, and suppliers.
*   **Contact & Address Books:** Maintain multiple contacts and addresses for each company, with automatic map link generation for logistical planning.
*   **Sales Pipeline Management:** Track sales opportunities from lead to closure using a visual Kanban board.
*   **Quotes & Proposals:** Create, send, and manage sales quotes. Convert accepted quotes directly into sales orders with a single click.
*   **Order Management:** Process and manage sales orders, with a clear view of order details, line items, and fulfillment status.
*   **Customer Invoicing:** Generate and manage customer invoices. Create invoices from sales orders or from scratch, and post them to the general ledger. You can also generate and download PDF copies for your customers.
*   **Discounts & Promotions:** Manage a wide range of pricing rules, from simple coupon codes to complex, multi-conditional discounts that are automatically applied to sales orders.
*   **Activity Tracking:** Log and manage all customer interactions, including calls, meetings, and tasks, to maintain a complete relationship history.
*   **E-commerce Integration:** Connect your online store to the ERP to automatically sync products, stock levels, and import new sales orders.
*   **Point of Sale (POS):** Process in-person transactions that atomically create all necessary sales, payment, and inventory records.
*   **Warranty & Asset Tracking:** Keep a record of all serialized products sold to customers and manage their warranty information.

### **Financial Accounting**

*   **General Ledger:** Manage your complete Chart of Accounts and post manual journal entries.
*   **Accounts Receivable (AR):**
    *   **Customer Payments:** Record and allocate payments received from customers.
    *   **Credit Notes:** Issue and apply credit notes to customer accounts.
    *   **Dunning & Collections:** Automatically identify overdue invoices and manage the dunning process.
    *   **AR Aging Reports:** Generate reports to analyze outstanding customer receivables by age.
*   **Accounts Payable (AP):**
    *   **Supplier Invoices:** Record and manage invoices received from suppliers.
    *   **Payment Processing:** Make individual payments or process bulk payment runs to multiple suppliers, including SEPA file generation for European banks.
    *   **AP Aging Reports:** Generate reports to monitor outstanding debts to suppliers.
*   **Financial Reporting:** Generate essential financial statements, including Profit & Loss, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow Forecasts.
*   **Budgeting:** Create and manage budgets, and generate reports that compare budgeted amounts against actual performance.
*   **Tax Management:** Configure tax rates and generate tax reports (e.g., VAT returns), with the ability to export them in formats required by tax authorities (like ELSTER XML).
*   **Multi-Company Consolidation:** Run financial consolidation processes across multiple business entities.
*   **Banking & Reconciliation:** Manage company bank accounts, import bank statements, and reconcile transactions against invoices and journal entries.

### **Fixed Asset Management**

*   **Asset Registry:** Maintain a complete register of all your company's fixed assets, from vehicles to machinery.
*   **Depreciation:** Automatically calculate and run depreciation for your assets using various methods (e.g., Straight-Line).
*   **Asset Maintenance:** Schedule and log all maintenance activities performed on assets to keep a complete history.

### **Inventory & Warehouse Management**

*   **Product Catalog:** Maintain a detailed catalog of all your products and services, organized by categories.
*   **Stock Level Tracking:** Get a real-time view of inventory levels across all your warehouses.
*   **Warehouse Operations:** Manage multiple warehouses, perform stock adjustments, and process inventory movements.
*   **Lot & Serial Number Tracking:** Trace the complete lifecycle of products by tracking them with unique serial numbers or lot numbers.
*   **Stocktaking:** Plan and conduct physical inventory counts, with the system automatically generating variance reports and stock adjustments.
*   **Landed Costs:** Accurately calculate inventory value by applying additional costs like freight, duties, and insurance to incoming stock.

### **Procurement & Purchasing**

*   **Supplier Database:** Maintain a comprehensive directory of your suppliers and their information.
*   **Purchase Orders:** Create, send, and track purchase orders with suppliers.
*   **Purchase Requisitions:** Allow internal teams to request purchases for approval before a formal purchase order is created.

### **Manufacturing**

*   **Bill of Materials (BOMs):** Define and manage multi-level bills of materials for your manufactured products.
*   **Work Orders:** Create, manage, and track the entire production process with work orders.
*   **Production Control:** Issue components to production, consume raw materials, and receive finished goods into inventory.
*   **Material Requirements Planning (MRP):** Run MRP to automatically calculate material demands and receive suggestions for new purchase and work orders.
*   **Work Centers & Routings:** Define your production lines, work centers, and the sequence of operations (routings) needed to manufacture products.

### **Human Resources (HR)**

*   **Employee Directory:** Maintain a central and secure record of all employee information.
*   **Organizational Management:** Define your company's structure by managing departments and job positions.
*   **Contract Management:** Store and manage employee work contracts within the system.
*   **Absence & Leave Management:** Manage all types of employee leave, from initial request to approval.
*   **Recruitment & Applicant Tracking:** Create job requisitions and manage the entire hiring pipeline using a visual Kanban board.
*   **Performance Management:** Track employee performance, conduct reviews, and manage performance improvement plans.
*   **Payroll:** Initiate and manage payroll runs for your employees.
*   **Employee Self-Service:** Allow employees to access their own information, such as downloading their payslips.

### **Project Management**

*   **Project Planning & Tracking:** Create and manage projects, track progress, and monitor key performance indicators.
*   **Task Management:** Assign and track tasks within projects to ensure accountability and progress.
*   **Time Tracking & Timesheets:** Log and manage time spent by employees on different projects and tasks for accurate costing and billing.

### **Logistics & Supply Chain**

*   **Carrier Management:** Configure and manage the shipping carriers you work with.
*   **Shipment Management:** Create and manage outbound shipments, link them to sales orders, and manage carrier information.
*   **Shipment Tracking:** Track the status of shipments with real-time updates directly from carrier systems.
*   **Fleet & Field Service Management:** Manage your fleet of vehicles, schedule service appointments for technicians, and create service reports.

### **Quality & Returns Management**

*   **Quality Control:** Create detailed inspection plans and perform quality checks on incoming, in-process, or finished goods.
*   **Return Management (RMA):** Manage the entire customer return process, from creating the Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) to receiving items and issuing credit notes.

### **Subscription Billing**

*   **Subscription & Plan Management:** Create and manage various subscription plans for your services.
*   **Recurring Billing:** Manage customer subscriptions and automatically run billing jobs to generate recurring invoices.
*   **Usage-Based Billing:** Report and bill for metered usage for applicable subscription plans.
*   **Revenue Reporting:** Generate reports to track key subscription metrics like Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR).

### **Collaboration & Communication**

*   **Unified Inbox:** Manage communications from various channels (e.g., email) in one central inbox. Assign messages to team members and link them to relevant records like customers or sales orders.
*   **Notifications:** Receive in-app notifications for important events, such as when a task is assigned to you or a document requires your approval.

### **Support & Helpdesk**

*   **Ticket Management:** Manage customer support requests through a complete ticketing system. Track ticket status, priority, and assignments.
*   **Service Level Agreements (SLAs):** Define and monitor SLA policies to ensure timely responses and resolutions for your customers.

### **System Administration & Customization**

*   **User & Role Management:** Control what each user can see and do with a powerful role-based permission system.
*   **Security:** Enforce strong security with features like Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
*   **UI Customization:** Adapt the user interface to different roles by defining UI profiles that can hide or show specific fields and elements.
*   **Customization:** Add custom fields, define document numbering sequences, and customize UI themes.
*   **Document Templates:** Create and manage custom templates for documents like invoices and quotes.
*   **Data Import:** Easily import large amounts of data, such as customers or products, using guided templates and data validation.
*   **Document Management:** Upload, store, and link files to any record in the system.
*   **Global Search:** Find any record—be it a customer, invoice, or product—from a single, powerful search bar.
*   **Integrations:** Connect the ERP to other applications using API keys and webhooks.
*   **Audit Trail:** Track key changes and actions performed by users throughout the system for security and compliance.
